Who we are

Named for Selene.
Built to enlighten.

Selenix Co. takes its name from Selene — the Greek goddess of the moon, whose light guides the way through darkness. That is precisely what we do for businesses.

We are not just a technology company. We are a full-spectrum business solutions studio — helping startups and established companies alike find clarity in strategy, stand out in their markets, and grow with confidence.

Founded by a team of driven, multidisciplinary professionals, we bring together expertise in technology, marketing, finance, operations, and community to deliver solutions that truly transform.
